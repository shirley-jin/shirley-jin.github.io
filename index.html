Assistant Professor in Social Entrepreneurship

Welcome to my website! I’m an Assistant Professor in School of Economics at Utrecht University. My research interests span across several fields: social entrepreneurship through the lens of organizational legitimacy and consumer behavior; computational methods including topic modeling and computer vision for organizational phenomena; emerging technologies as an enabler of entrepreneurship.
